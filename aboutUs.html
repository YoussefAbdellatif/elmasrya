we are Massria group 100 % egyptian fund consist of three different companies as followed :-

1.Massria Mechanical Parts Company (MMPC)

- This company established in 1998 as trading co. in the field of importing earthmoving equipment spare parts as (CAT, Komatsu, John Deere, asphalt plant and crushers) - 1999 we expand our activity to new area sewer networks hand tools (Sewer rods – sewer cleaning machine with cables – sewer plugs) - 2000 we decided to cover all the sewer networks service machines for sewer & drainage lines as we added vacuum trucks, jet trucks, combo jet / Vac trucks, CCTV systems pipe line inspections) - 2004 as our management interested in the environmental and keep it clean, we moved to new goal street & runway sweepers added to our equipment lines to our local market -2008 we add trenchless pipe rehabilitation as pioneer green solution for the sewer lines networks aged, broken, sealing pipes -2009 we add centrifugal pumps for drinking & sewer water to our equipment lines
