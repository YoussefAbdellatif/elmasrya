Learn More About Who We Are!


we are Massria group 100 % egyptian fund consist of three different companies as followed :-

1.Massria Mechanical Parts Company (MMPC)

- This company established in 1998 as trading co. in the field of importing earthmoving equipment spare parts as (CAT, Komatsu, John Deere, asphalt plant and crushers)
- 1999 : we expand our activity to new area sewer networks hand tools (Sewer rods – sewer cleaning machine with cables – sewer plugs)
- 2000 : we decided to cover all the sewer networks service machines for sewer & drainage lines as we added vacuum trucks, jet trucks, combo jet / Vac trucks, CCTV systems pipe line inspections)
- 2004 : as our management interested in the environmental and keep it clean, we moved to new goal street & runway sweepers added to our equipment lines to our local market
-2008 : we add trenchless pipe rehabilitation as pioneer green solution for the sewer lines networks aged, broken, sealing pipes
-2009 : we add centrifugal pumps for drinking & sewer water to our equipment lines

2- Massria Measuring & Control System company(MMECO)

2014: as part from our government encouraging local industries, we shift to fuel fill such goal by our new company in the field of smart water and electric meter. Under technical super vision and cooperation with our global suppliers.

3 – D& A Firma

- 2020 : We established our new sister company in Freising, Germany as trading co. for expanding our supply chain to African countries in the following fields:
1- sewer & water pipe lines equipment’s.
2- Cosmetics
3- Pharmaceutical
